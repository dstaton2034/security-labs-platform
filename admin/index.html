How it works

STEP 01
You describe the lab
Type something like "Walk a new hire through isolating a compromised device in MDE" or paste in a product doc.
STEP 02
AI builds step-by-step instructions
Azure AI Foundry generates numbered steps, real portal navigation paths, copyable KQL queries, expected results, and hints — all based on the actual product.
STEP 03
Employee follows along in their real environment
No sandbox needed. Each step has a checkbox — employees check off as they go, with auto-progression and a completion badge when done.
STEP 04
Publish to the portal
Review, approve, and publish with one click. The lab appears for every employee — searchable, trackable, and always current.
